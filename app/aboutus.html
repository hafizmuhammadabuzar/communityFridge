About Us

Community Fridge - Global Data Base Of Sharing is an application that will provide a global database for sharing, which helps individuals to locate any sharing platform nearby their location. Current version have three types which includes Fridge, Water Cooler & Shelf Beside it enables individual users, groups & restaurants to share their food or any other useful items by using our app.

App provides you a number of smart options to help & locate nearby fridges, their status and food requirements.

Community Fridge - Global Data Base Of Sharing has been commissioned and developed by Synergistics. An app development company based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. For further information please visit www.synergistics.ae
